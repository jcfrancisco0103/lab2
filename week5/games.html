Games I Play

VALORANT

VALORANT is a tactical first-person shooter game developed and published by Riot Games. It features a cast of unique characters known as "Agents," each with their own special abilities. The game is known for its competitive gameplay and strategic team-based action.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to build and explore virtual worlds made up of blocks. It has various gameplay modes, including survival mode where players must acquire resources and maintain health, and creative mode where players have unlimited resources and can build freely.

Left 4 Dead 2

Left 4 Dead 2 is a cooperative first-person shooter game where players fight against hordes of zombie-like infected. The game features intense action, strategic gameplay, and a variety of weapons to survive the zombie apocalypse.
